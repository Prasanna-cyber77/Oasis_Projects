About Him

Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam was one of India's most respected scientists and leaders. Known as the Missile Man of India, he played a key role in India's defense and space programs and later served as the 11th President of India.

Major Achievements

ISRO

Led India's first Satellite Launch Vehicle program.

Pokhran-II

Played a vital role in India's nuclear test program.

President

Served as the 11th President of India from 2002-2007.
